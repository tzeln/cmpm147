Description

Multiple different images can be selected to display a colorizing effect. With good results, the active image should be reminiscent of a mosaic of sorts. All requirements are met for this assignment.

Technical

In order to get color from the original inspiration, I used image.get(x,y) and made alterations to the bounds to always sample the middle of the randomly generated rectangle. Additionally, instead of picking random spots within the canvas, I pick random spots within intervals of the canvas in hopes of increasing the accuracy of the final image. To make sure there's less color variation in each generation, I've lowered the possible standard deviation in the function colorMut.

Annealing

Increase the accuracy of generations by slowly moving the slider from right to left.

Reflection

Honestly the best assignment I've had so far, I just wish the results were a bit better. I'm unsure how to actually achieve those results without actually making some sort of image parsing function that recognizes points of high interest/contrast though and emphasizes that they are accurate in separate generations.
